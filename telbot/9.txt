Druman
Attribute: Pyrus, Darkus Power: Unknown
(Japanese version Keble (キーブル, Kīburu)) is a Pyrus and Darkus hybrid Bakugan. His body is a little bit more Pyrus than Darkus and his wings are more Darkus than Pyrus. His wings are hand with eyes on them and has a reptilian body.He, with Centorrior, are sent by Naga to oppose Dan and the Battle Brawlers. Druman is the more battle happy of the two.
