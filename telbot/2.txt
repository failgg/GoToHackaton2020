Blade Tigrerra
Attribute: Haos Power:450-700
(Japanese version: Blade Tigress (ブレードティグレス?)) is a Bakugan and the evolved form of Tigrerra. She is the Guardian Bakugan of Runo Misaki.Blade Tigrerra evolved from Tigrerra. Standing on its hind legs, Blade Tigrerra can judge the right moment to pounce on foes. Blades all over Tigrerra's body gleam with menace to intimidate her foes. When provoked, Blade Tigrerra's strong, sharp fangs can shred an opponent in an instant. Blade Tigrerra is Runo's guardian.
