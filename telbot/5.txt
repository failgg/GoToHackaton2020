Clayf
Attribute: Subterra Power: 500-700
(クレイフ, Kureifu) was a Bakugan and one of the six Legendary Soldiers of Vestroia. He was the soldier of Subterra.Clayf is one of The Six Legendary Soldiers of Vestroia and is the strongest of all the six soldiers. It battles with a large, multi-pronged lochaber axe that also works as a shield. It absorbs any ability that is given to its opponents during battle.
