Cycloid
Attribute: Subterra Power:370-580
(サイクロプス, Saikuropusu, Japanese version: Cyclops) is a cyclops-like Bakugan equipped with a large hammer made of stone. In the anime, he is the Guardian Bakugan of Billy Gilbert.Cycloid is a titan-like beast. He is incredibly strong, tough and eager for the fight. He has a single terrifying eye with a short deadly horn on his forehead and two vampire-like teeth. He finds humans both confusing and amusing.
