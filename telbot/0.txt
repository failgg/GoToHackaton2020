Apollonir
Attribute: Pyrus Power: 500-700
(Japanese version: Fafnir (ファーブニル, Fābuniru)) was the most powerful Dragonoid Bakugan and the leader of the Legendary Soldiers of Vestroia. In Bakugan: New Vestroia when Drago was defeated and taken by Spectra Phantom, he became Dan's temporary Guardian Bakugan to help him win Drago back.
